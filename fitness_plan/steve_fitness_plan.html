06 The Non-Negotiables
Rule 01
Protein First
Hit 180g protein every single day — training or not. This protects your 130 lbs of muscle mass while losing 20 lbs of fat. Everything else is secondary.
Rule 02
Kitchen Closes
8pm hard stop. This single habit eliminates the majority of unplanned snacking and sweet treats. Brush teeth immediately after dinner if needed.
Rule 03
Same Wake Time
7am every day including weekends. This is the anchor of your circadian rhythm and the foundation of sleep quality — more impactful than any supplement.
Rule 04
Snack Intentionally
One planned snack at 5:30pm from the pre-approved list. Not spontaneous, not multiple. Sweet cravings get a protein-first response before anything sweet.
Rule 05
Fuel the Work
Don't cut calories on hard training days. Eat more on ride and lift days (2,200–2,400 cal), less on rest days (2,000). Let training drive the deficit, not starvation.
Rule 06
Retest FTP
Week 8 FTP test is mandatory. Every zone, every workout, every phase of the training plan depends on this number being accurate and current.
